About Me

Who Am I?

Hello! I’m Rein! as mentioned before, I'm a 1st year student taking the BSEMC program at CIIT College of Arts and Technology. I currently reside in Quezon City but come from Cavite down south! For most of my life i've been exposed to games and music for most of my life but have expanded in hobbies since then.

I've taken interest in literature particularly in poetry, game development, programming, fitness and ought to expand even more! I truly believe that the mere enjoyment in partaking in a hobby is more than enough to warrant the time spent in it. However, I aspire to be a Video Game Composer/Game Developer and while there's a long road ahead of me, i'm more than willing to go through the hoops for such a career!
