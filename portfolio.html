My Music

This is my work related to my musical journey, featuring works from my band "Beatsilog" and solo compositions under the name "Myst_iU". This serves as a testament to my growth and experience as a musician and recording artist over the past five years.

Sunday Last June

Beatsilog

The band's biggest and (most expensive) production. Personally, this is one of those songs that I wish I could've repeated. While it was the band's most effortful work, it proved to be more difficult than we expected. The production rush, working with a production team, and the expectations being at an all-time high since this was our first song to be released under our previous record label, DITTO Philippines, taught me to appreciate the simplicity of songwriting. I now consider the entire experience a wake-up call to improve the other aspects of becoming a recording artist.

Bawat Saglit

Beatsilog

My most memorable composition, this song was first conceived in late 2019 when I was experiencing a depressing time in my life. Initially, it was a love song dedicated to a certain someone, but it quickly took a gloomy turn when things didn’t go as expected. Still, without that challenging time in my life, this song wouldn't have turned out the way it did.

ily.

Beatsilog

Our band's most popular song! While I wasn't as involved in the production of this song, only contributing to the early composition process and adding extra instrumentals like synths (I don't even appear in the music video due to quarantine restrictions at the time), it remains one of my favorite songs to listen to. There's something about its catchy hook and addictive chorus that I've come to love.

Selene

Beatsilog

More of my newer compositions. At this point in time, I've developed and matured as an artist and have moved past the idea that complicated music equates to good music. For once, I felt a sense of artistic peace. The song's title, "Selene," was inspired by the naming conventions of formidable jazz artists who used feminine names for their songs or albums.

Pagkakamali

Beatsilog

The band's first-ever music video! This was a very fun project to work on, and I consider it to be the discovery stage for most of the band, including myself. The composition was also a sequel to "Bawat Saglit." While the two songs are not connected canonically, the videos share similar themes of "romantic break-up," though this one leans more into the frustrations and anger that follow being wronged. This was also a time when the band was quite shy about our musical capabilities, and as a result, I took the lead in composing the song on my own. I'm glad that it's not the case anymore.

Myst_iU · I still love you.

I still love you.

Myst_iU

One of my favorites from my very early works, this song wasn't so much a test of my songwriting capabilities as it was a test of my production skills. Let's just say I still had quite a bit to learn. I could go on all day about how much I dislike certain aspects of it, such as the instruments I used, the production, and the samples, but I learned that we all start somewhere. My critical view of my old work is one of the strongest signs that I've grown as a musician.

My Code

This is a collection of programming-related work I've created over the years.
Featuring a variety of personal and academic projects, including Java and C++, these highlight my journey, the skills and experience i have developed over the years. Feel free to explore these projects to gain insight into the my abilities and approach to programming.

Simple ATM Machine (with functions)

C++

A simple ATM Machine program that has been revised to incorporate and show the utility of functions in C++ and programming as a whole.

Using Loops in Math Program

C++

A simple program that uses operators and loops to be able to display and create the Fibonacci Sequence, Pascal's Triangle, and also find the Prime Number/s of a particular number all based on the input that's given by the user.

Reading And Saving User Information Program

C++

Similar to the POS System the following program displays the functionality of "ifstream" and "ofstream" and how it can be used to store and read data of a .txt file outside of the program.

Point Of Sales System

C++

The following project is a simple Point Of Sales System that showcases the functionality of "ifstream" and "ofstream" to be able to store and read data. It has the cashier and administrator functionality. One is able to make a transaction and reflect the following transaction in the data that has been stored and the other, has the capability to add and remove "products" that are listed where it is stored.
